Overview

Putri Minesweeper is a simple puzzle game designed to be played directly on your device. Based on the current app implementation, the app focuses on local gameplay and settings customization.

Putri Minesweeper does not require account registration and does not ask users to submit personal details in order to play.

Information We Collect

Putri Minesweeper does not intentionally collect personal information such as your full name, phone number, postal address, or payment details through the app itself.

The app may store basic gameplay-related preferences on your device, such as local game settings, in order to support the app experience. This information stays on the device unless you choose to remove the app or clear app data.

How Information Is Used

Any local app data is used only to support core app functionality, such as:

  • maintaining game configuration such as board size and mine count,
  • supporting gameplay flow and user experience,
  • keeping the app functioning properly on your device.

Data Retention and Deletion

Putri Minesweeper does not currently create user accounts or maintain a backend service for gameplay data. Based on the current app implementation, gameplay preferences and similar app data remain on your device until you remove the app or clear the app's local storage through your device settings.

Because no account-based personal data is maintained by the current version of the app, there is no separate server-side account deletion workflow at this time.

Data Sharing

Putri Minesweeper does not sell personal information and does not intentionally share personal information with third parties through the app's core gameplay functionality.

If future versions add analytics, advertisements, cloud storage, or account systems, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those features are released.

Children's Privacy

Putri Minesweeper is a general audience game. The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a future version of the app, please contact the app owner so appropriate action can be taken.

Security

Reasonable efforts are taken to keep the app implementation straightforward and minimize unnecessary data handling. Because no system can guarantee absolute security, users should also keep their devices updated and protected.

In the current version, the app is designed for local gameplay and does not rely on user account login or remote storage for core use. If future versions introduce network-based features or third-party services, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those changes are released.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or operational updates. The latest published version on this page should be treated as the current version.
